Competition Type

Prime Body Championship Competition is a premier multi-discipline fitness championship featuring bodybuilding, physique, strength, and functional fitness events. The competition welcomes both amateur and professional athletes from across the region. Men and women aged 18 years and above who are passionate about fitness and strength sports are eligible to participate. The event aims to promote a healthy lifestyle, celebrate athletic excellence, and provide a platform for competitors to showcase their dedication, discipline, and performance.

Categories

Body Building

Bodybuilding is the discipline of using progressive resistance exercise—primarily weightlifting—combined with high-protein nutrition and rest to maximize muscle hypertrophy, symmetry, and definition. Focused on aesthetics rather than pure strength, it is a demanding lifestyle requiring immense dedication to training and diet to achieve a sculpted,, low-body-fat physique.

Men’s Physique

Men’s Physique is a bodybuilding division prioritizing an aesthetic, athletic, and symmetrical "beach body" rather than extreme mass. Key features include a wide shoulder-to-waist taper, well-developed chest, and capped shoulders, paired with visible abdominal definition. It emphasizes a "total package" look, wearing board shorts rather than posing trunks

Women Fitness

Women’s fitness is a holistic journey to enhance physical strength, mental clarity, and longevity through tailored exercise like strength training, cardio, and flexibility routines. It empowers women across life stages—addressing hormonal changes from pregnancy to menopause—to improve bone density, manage stress, and build confidence.

CrossFit

CrossFit is a high-intensity fitness program designed to improve overall physical health through constantly varied, functional movements—such as lifting, running, and gymnastics—executed at high intensity. It combines Olympic weightlifting, cardio, and calisthenics in group "box" settings, emphasizing strength, agility, and community support.

Powerlifting

Powerlifting is a competitive strength sport focused on maximizing weight lifted in three compound movements: the squat, bench press, and deadlift. Athletes compete across weight and age classes, attempting to achieve the highest total combined weight from three attempts at each lift, evaluated by judges for strict form.
