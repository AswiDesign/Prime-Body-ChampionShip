Body Buliding

Bodybuilding evaluates overall muscular development, symmetry, conditioning, and stage presentation.
Eligibility
Open to male and female athletes aged 18 years and above competing within designated weight classes.
Judging Criteria:
- Overall muscle mass and density
- Proportion and symmetry
- Conditioning and definition
- Mandatory poses execution
- Stage presence and presentation

Men's Physique

Men’s Physique emphasizes an aesthetic, athletic upper-body with balanced proportions and stage confidence.
Eligibility:
Open to male athletes aged 18 years and above competing within height categories.
Judging Criteria:
- Shoulder-to-waist ratio (V-taper)
- Upper-body symmetry and shape
- Muscle tone and conditioning
- Posture and confidence
- Overall aesthetic presentation

Women Fitness

Women Fitness assesses a toned, athletic physique with symmetry, balance, and stage elegance.
Eligibility
Open to female athletes aged 18 years and above competing within height categories.
Judging Criteria:
- Muscle tone and firmness
- Overall symmetry and proportion
- Stage walk and poise
- Confidence and presentation
- Healthy athletic appearance

CrossFit

CrossFit Challenge evaluates functional fitness through strength, endurance, agility, and work capacity.
Eligibility:
Open to male and female athletes aged 18 years and above with functional training experience.
Judging Criteria:
- Strength performance
- Cardiovascular endurance
- Movement efficiency
- Workout completion time
- Technical execution

Powerlifting

Powerlifting measures maximum strength across three primary lifts: squat, bench press, and deadlift.
Eligibility
Open to male and female athletes aged 18 years and above competing within official weight classes.
Judging Criteria:
- Maximum successful lift per discipline
- Combined total weight lifted
- Technical compliance with lift standards
- Control and completion of movement
- Official judge validation
